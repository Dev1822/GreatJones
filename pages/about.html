Meet Great Jones

We're a women-run, New York-based small business that wants to equip and empower you to cook at home — and make your kitchen your happy place.


The power and pleasure of cooking at home

Whether you’re baking sourdough or scrambling eggs in your pajamas, you’re doing it. You’re cooking. And, Great Jones is here to help. We want you to feel equipped and empowered to cook at home with designs that look as fantastic as they function.


Our Inspiration: Judith Jones

The name Great Jones is an homage to Judith Jones, a cookbook author and editor who championed the work of Julia Child, Edna Lewis, and James Beard, among many others. She treated her cookware like art. It’s also a nod to New York City — which we’re proud to call home.
